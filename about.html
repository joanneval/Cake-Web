About Asuncion's Purple Hub

Asuncion's Purple Hub is a home-based bakery dedicated to creating delicious and beautifully designed cakes for every special occasion. What started as a simple passion for baking has grown into a small business focused on spreading happiness through sweet creations.

We specialize in freshly baked cakes made with quality ingredients and absolutely no preservatives. Each cake is carefully crafted with love, creativity, and attention to detail to ensure both taste and design meet our customer's expectations.

Cake Handling Guidelines

  1. OUR CAKE HAS NO PRESERVATIVES
    It's made of a lot of milk, Ube, and LOVE! ingredients like milk, ube, and lots of LOVE!
  2. IT IS EASILY SPOILED
    You must refrigerate it right a way!
  3. WE RECOMMENED TO CONSUME IT IMMEDIATELY
    Only a maximum of 2 hours without refrigeration for transport. Always beware especially on a warm/hot environment.
  4. IF NOT SURE ON HOW LONG THE CAKE HAS BEEN SITTING OUTSIDE, CHECK IT BEFORE CONSUMING.
    Smell, or see for any signs of spoilage before consumption.
